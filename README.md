# Mechanical Manuals Archive

## What this is
I'm collecting and uploading old manuals for things like chainsaws, lawn mowers, small engines, vehicles, and whatever else I come across.

A lot of this stuff is getting harder to find, especially older equipment, and I don’t want it disappearing.

## What this is NOT
This is not a piracy site.

I'm not trying to take anything away from companies or sell their work. This is mainly for:
- old manuals
- stuff that’s discontinued
- things that are a pain to find online

If something here is still being sold or shouldn't be up, let me know and I’ll take it down.

## Why I'm doing this
Because it sucks when you have a machine that works fine but you can't find the manual anywhere.

Fixing stuff > throwing it away.

## Contributing
If you’ve got manuals lying around, feel free to add them.

Just try to:
- name files clearly
- put them in the right place
- don’t upload stuff that’s easily available for purchase

## Final note
Use this info at your own risk. Don’t do anything sketchy or unsafe just because a manual says so.
